# Chinese landing-page visual sources

The product screenshots in this directory were captured from a locally running Lumilio Photos instance after importing the demo dataset under `server/data/storage/primary/landing-demo`.

The scene images are local WebP derivatives of Unsplash photographs used in that demo dataset:

- `scene-lake.webp`: Unsplash photo `photo-1470770841072-f978cf4d019e`
- `scene-beach.webp`: Unsplash photo `photo-1507525428034-b723cf961d3e`
- `scene-fox.webp`: Unsplash photo `photo-1474511320723-9a56873867b5`
- `scene-bird.webp`: Unsplash photo `photo-1444464666168-49d633b86797`
- `scene-portrait.webp`: Unsplash photo `photo-1524504388940-b1c1722653e1`
- `scene-camera.webp`: Unsplash photo `photo-1516035069371-29a1b244cc32`

Source photography is used under the Unsplash License. The full demo-dataset manifest records the remaining photos and locally derived OCR, face-clustering, burst, and near-duplicate test images.
